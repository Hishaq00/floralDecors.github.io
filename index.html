About us

Our team at The Floral Decor have garnered an illustrious reputation for elevating weddings and events through the beauty of flowers. Underpinned by a passion for floral artistry, The Floral Decor was founded by Freeda in 2019 and our team have since worked with couples across the Capital and beyond to curate flowers from the heart. Focusing on natural, romantic and timeless flowers, our signature style evolves around your love story.
